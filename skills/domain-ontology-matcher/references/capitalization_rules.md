## Title Case Capitalization Rules

When converting text to title case, you must lowercase specific connecting words unless they are the first or last word of the title.

### Instruction

Capitalize the first letter of every word, EXCEPT for the following lowercase words:

- Articles: a, an, the
- Coordinating Conjunctions: and, but, or, nor, for, yet, so
- Short Prepositions: as, at, by, for, in, of, on, per, to, up, via
- Subordinating Conjunctions (when used as connectors): as, if, than, that

### Examples for context:

- "The Lord of the Rings" (NOT "The Lord Of The Rings")
- "To Be or Not to Be" (NOT "To Be Or Not To Be")
- "Gone with the Wind" (NOT "Gone With The Wind")
