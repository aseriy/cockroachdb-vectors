# Domain and Ontology Matcher

## Workflow

1. **Research**: Given a company/account name, research what their business is
2. **Present findings**: Offer back what you found about their business
3. **Conversation**: Ask about specific product, team, org, use cases, etc. to augment the understanding
4. **Identify**: Determine which semantic domains and ontologies are relevant
5. **Query**: Run database scripts to retrieve domain and ontology data
6. **Output**: Return results (format TBD)

## Available Domains and Ontologies

Naming pattern: `<domain>.<ontology>`

Retrieved via database scripts.
